RJ45 & XLR Pin Chart

You can use the pin detail below to make adapter between ESTA standard RJ45, LOR RJ45 and XLR connectors.

Signal RJ45 XLR
ESTA LOR Renard 3 Pin 5 Pin
Data + 1 4 5 3 3
Data - 2 5 4 2 2
Data Common 7 6 1+2 1 1
